Moving Posters

Expressing identity
through type, shapes, and motion

The Challenge

Develop a contemporary way of communicating myself as a designer.

The Inspiration

I’ve seen a ton of amazing kinetic typography and motion design on Instagram—and I saw an opportunity to make my own.

The Outcome

Each visual element, typography, and motion nested within its poster represented each facet of who I am as a designer.

The Future

I enjoy making these. I see myself continuing this type of design and uploading it on social media and design websites.
