Swish

The Problem

The lack of empathy and human interaction in online shopping services

The Solution

Create a browser extension and a mobile application to provide shoppers a better shopping experience

Overview

Swish is a browser and mobile application extentsion that creates a better online shopping experience. One of Swish’s key features is that it collects and compiles all your wishlisted items across all online stores you are logged into.

Inspiration

I created swish because I mostly do my purchases online. Recently, I bought a pair of shoes and after receiving them, I felt a strong sense of buyers remorse despite all the research and decision making I had prior to the purchase. This made me realize that even though online shopping has become seamless and easy to use over the years, it does not mean that there isn’t room for any improvement.


The features that Swish provides came from the idea of solving my own (and the people I interviewed) personal issues and concerns about online shopping.

Defining
The Problem

  • Shoppers prematurely purchase Items before it goes on sale
  • No platform to compare prices
  • Shoppers miss their parcel's
    delivery date
  • Hidden custom and duty fees

Prioritising
The Solutions

  • Cross Website Comparisons

    Build a Universal Wishlist. It compiles all your items across all your wishlists into one singular list.

  • Missing out promotions and Sales

    Notifying users about promotions and deals + Automatically switches items if items is cheaper.

  • Low Stock / Sold out item

    Notification if stock is low and back in stock + and if sold out, the application recommends same product from different store.

  • Missing Deliveries

    Detailed delivery tracking + ability to leave not to service provider, pre-authorization & ability to pay for shipping + duty fees prior to delivery.

  • Hidden customs & duty fees

    Customs and Duty fees are calculated  and provided estimated into the total.

  • Confusing return and exchange policies

    Quick & easy access to return/exchange policies from sellers. + notification of return/exchange expiration date.
