Milya

A digital home for families separated
by long distances.

The Challenge

Investigate and find what OFW families’s go through and what they wish for.

The Inspiration

Personal experience. Remembering the past experiences I had and what technology could have done to improved my life as a son of a OFW father.

The Outcome

A concept app that aims to improve family relationships. It also works as a tool to help OFW families keep in track of their financial life.

The Future

Instead of trying to fix several problems, I would have focused more on one issue and solve it more in detail.

Research

General Research

By gathering academic papers, respectable sources, and interviewing, I was able to collect the most recurring issues of OFW families.

Information Architecture

From there, features were born based on each problem. An information architecture to map out the features.

Persona

A family persona was created in order to understand the user flow and user journey of the application.

 
 
 

What is Milya?

 
 
 
 

A Family Messenger

Milya’s messenger is build with an idea of serving families separated by long distances.

A Bill Tracker & Money Transferer

Manage your bills easily by organising and tracking all your transactions within the app.

A Family Hub

Never miss a birthday, a graduation, or your son’s first day of school. Milya has a centralised calendar where all family members can see and edit events.

Brand Identity

 
 
 
 

Sign In

Family & Moments

Family

This is where families can organise their accounts and where they can find their files such as: media, books, documents, history, and etc.

Moments

Here, each member of the family can see what’s happening in their lives.

 
 

Messenger

Milya’s messenger is built on the idea of capturing the way family members interact with each other.

Features like sending money and gifts for birthdays and reading bed time stories are just some of the ways Milya can help improve family relationships.

 
 
 
 
 
 

Wallet

Managing funds is extremely important to a family household. Having to pay for bills could be difficult especially when you are thousands of miles away.

With Milya, money management is easier than ever. You can pay for tuition, mortgages, hydro and etc. There’s even an online gift shop where you can buy gift cards and credits and send them to you love ones.
