The Challenge

Improving experiences by solving issues related to online shopping.

The Inspiration

Missing out on sale because I was not notified about it and having a terrible experience returning a purchase online.

The Outcome

A browser extension and application that complies all your saved items and it’s feature to purchase and return/exchange within the application.

The Future

A conceptual design that could potentially be a feature within an existing company like Pinterest.

Research


Prioritising Solutions

Through interviews, I found out the common issues online shoppers find. These issues were then later filtered to which issues reoccur the most.

Information Architecture

Through prioritising solutions, an information structure was built. These were categorised based on related information.
