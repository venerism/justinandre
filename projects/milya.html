Profile

Milya

The Challenge

Create a digital platform focused on long distance family relationships.

The Outcome

A digital platform that provides emotional connectivity and practical use between users.

Overview

Milya, or short for “pamilya” (family in filipino) is a multi-function messenger oriented towards families that are separated by financial instability. It is intended for Overseas Foreign Workers (OFW),who need a platform to connect with their families emotionally and for practical reseaons.

Inspiration

My inspiration is based on my personal experience. When I was younger, my father had to leave me and my family to work as an architect in Qatar. Those years were rough in a way, If a product like Milya existed then, it would’ve made my families life a bit easier.

Defining

The Problem

  • Boys look for male role models like in most traditional family structures (although not required).

  • Birthdays, holidays and special occasions like: graduations, sports events and etc. are difficult to compress how much they miss each other.

  • Difficulty expressing their love for each other because sometimes words are not enough.

  • OFW children miss their mother’s (or father’s) home cooking.

  • OFW children feel uncertain why their parents left in the first place, despite economic evidence.

  • OFW children sometimes feel abandoned   and this results them to act rebelious.

  • Safety concerns for both parties.

  • OFW parents may feel that their children does not recognize them anymore

  • OFW parents could use a better financial management device that can accurately track where they spend their money.

  • Time differences makes it hard to communicate with each other.

  • Technical difficulties such as slow internet connection and close-to-obsolete devices are still being used.
